The Rules

  • Players can move north,south,west,east or up (creating a new block).
  • Players can only move onto an unoccupied block.
  • Neutral blocks can be captured from an adjacent block of equal or greater height.
  • Opposition blocks can be captured from an adjacent block of greater height.
  • The game ends when the whole cube is full.
